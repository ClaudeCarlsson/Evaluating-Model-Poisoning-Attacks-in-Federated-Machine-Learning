Target Label Flipping attack where all clients are poisoned.

There is data for 5 clients and 10 rounds, 10 clients and 20 rounds, and 10 clients and 40 rounds.
The attack is performed with between 1 and 10 targetet numbers (the numbers between 0 and 9). The targetet number/numbers will be switched to another number, whilst all other numbers will not be affected.
