Target Label Flipping attack where a percentage of clients are poisoned.

There is data for 10 clients and 20 rounds.
The attack is performed with between 1 and 10 targetet numbers (the numbers between 0 and 9). The targetet number/numbers will be switched to another number, whilst all other numbers will not be affected. Only a choosen percentage of the clients are affected.

The file names are called for example 10_client_10_5.json where 10_client explains the amount of clients that are being used, _10 means that 10% of clients are affected by the attack and _5 is the amount of targeted numbers.
